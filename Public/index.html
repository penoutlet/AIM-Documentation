What is AIM?



A.I.M. (AIM) is short for the Alexa Inventory Management application, developed by Dijatek LLC, and the Whittaker Group Inc. Like your smart phone, Alexa is an interface for interacting with applications. Just as you can open apps on your phone, you can open AIM with Alexa. AIM is an application developed to allow Alexa owners to shop and place orders using Alexa. The documentation for AIM lives on this site.
