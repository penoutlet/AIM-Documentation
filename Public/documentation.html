Is Alexa Listening?

To start AIM, say "Alexa, open AIM." This starts the AIM application and prompts Alexa to listen for commands.

  • You must get Alexa to listen with the command "Alexa, open AIM" prior to issuing any other command.
  • Alexa listens for 7 seconds before going dormant. Issue your command within this time limit to expand the time limit by an additional 7 seconds.
  • Alexa can listen for a maximum of 14 seconds.

Adding to Your Cart



Commands
"Add to cart."
"Add item number 3."
"Add an item to the cart."
"Add an item to my cart."

Issue one of the above commands to add an item to your cart. Note, you can add the item with two separate commands, or with one. The command “Alexa, add to cart,” will prompt Alexa to ask you which item to add. The command “Alexa, add item number [item number] to the cart.” will complete the task in one step.

Viewing Your Cart



Commands
"Display cart."
"Show cart."
"Show order."
"Display order."
"What’s in the cart?"
"What is my order?"
"What's in my cart?

To "view" your cart, issue one of the above commands to Alexa. For example, saying 'Show cart" once Alexa has begun listening will prompt Alexa to read aloud the contents of your cart.

Removing from Your Cart



Commands
"Remove an item"
"Remove item number 3"
"Delete an item."
"Delete item number 3."

To remove an item from your cart, issue one of the above commands. Saying “Alexa, remove an item” will prompt Alexa to ask you which item to remove. To remove an item without this prompt, say “Alexa, remove item number [item number].”


Finalizing Your Order



Commands
"Finish order."
"Finalize order."
"Complete order."
"Order finished."
"We're done."

Issue one of the above commands to finish your order. This will trigger Alexa to send an email to you. Open this email to get a link to your Amazon cart. From your amazon cart, you can edit the cart and place your order.
