Opening AIM

Sample commands:

“Alexa, open AIM.”

To start AIM, say "Alexa, open AIM." This also initiates a shopping cart. Once you’ve initiated a cart, you can begin shopping. Note, the device will allow no more than a 7 second wait time for a command. If the wait time is exceeded, you will have to say “Alexa, open AIM.” again. 

Adding to Your Cart

Sample commands:

"Add to cart."

"Add item number 3."

"Add an item to the cart."

"Add an item to my cart."

To add to the cart you made by opening AIM, issue one of the above commands. Note that you can add the item with two separate commands, or with one. The command “Alexa, add to cart,” will prompt Alexa to ask you which item to add. To complete the action with one command, say “Alexa, add item number [item number] to the cart.”

Viewing Your Cart

Sample commands:

"Display cart."

"Show cart."

"Show order."

"Display order."

"What’s in the cart?"

"What is my order?"

"What's in my cart?"

To "view" your cart, issue one of the above commands to Alexa. This will prompt Alexa to read aloud the contents of your cart.

Removing from Your Cart

Sample commands:

"Remove an item."

"Remove item number 3."

"Delete an item."

"Delete item number 3."

To remove an item from your cart, issue one of the above commands. Saying “Alexa, remove an item” will prompt Alexa to ask you which item to remove. To remove an item without this prompt, say “Alexa, remove item number [item number].”

Finalizing Your Order

Sample commands:

“Finish order.”

“Finalize order.”

“Complete order.”

“Order finished.”

“Order complete.”

“We’re done.”

Issue one of the above commands to finish your order. This will trigger Alexa to send an email to you. Open this email to get a link to your Amazon cart. From your amazon cart, you can edit the cart and place your order.
